# Keycaps

Most keys are 1u. The non-1u keys are:

**1.25u**
- ⎇ (ALT) - x2
- H
- G
- BLANK (ANY)

**1.5u**
- ⌃ (CTRL) - x2
- ⌘ (CMD) - x2
- ⇥ (TAB)
- | (PIPE)
- T
- N
- 7/&

**1.75u**
- ⎋ (ESC)
- ⇪ (CAPSLOCK)

**2u**
- ⇧ (LSHIFT)
- ⌫ (BACKSPACE)

**2.25u**
- ↵ (ENTER)

**2.75u**
- SPACE
- ⇧ (RSHIFT)
